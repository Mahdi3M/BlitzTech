Description

7 years ago today I built my first PC, parentally funded, and fully budget build. Now, one Bachelors's degree and pandemic later I'm running my dream PC.... and it can still barely run Star Citizen. Some things never change!

I built this PC during the Pandemic after waiting in lines and overnights amongst gamers, miners, and scalpers all fiending for a GPU. After many late nights (and beers with the boys) I finally had the missing piece to my build a Founders Edition RTX 3080.

This build was a lot of hard work and harder overnighters. To the people still looking for GPUs, don't give up!
